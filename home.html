Queen Bee Cafe


It's all started with my grandma Anna and her love to the bees which she shared with us. We are committed to living up her legacy of beekeeping.
That is why we opened our Queen Bee Farm Cafe in a little town of Melrose in Scottish Borders
To promote our beekeeping traditions and create new ones.


We created

A delicious breakfast and lunch menu with freshly baked cakes and pastries featuring local ingredients.

We are selling

Our organic honey and quality products from local artists.

We organise

A regular monthly bee keeping course to educated and help raise awareness of bees and other pollinators.
