Bees matter!

As pollinators, bees play a part in every aspect of the ecosystem. They support the growth of trees, flowers, and other plants, which serve as food and shelter for creatures large and small. A third of the world's food production depends on bees Without them, the availability and diversity of fresh produce would rapidly decline.

Seven ways to help bees:

  • Keep bee-friendly plants
  • Leave a dish of water out for bees to drink from it
  • Avoid using pesticides
  • Bee informed-Spread awareness
  • Support your local beekeepers
  • Join to our beekeeping course
  • Start your own Hive
